The who

I'm Mansour Mansour, an enthusiastic and passionate front-end developer living in Stockholm, Sweden. With over eight years of hands-on web development experience, I specialize in using JavaScript, CSS, and HTML together to develop solutions to online problems. I enjoy building beautiful, semantic, intuitive, and responsive websites using the latest technologies. I love what I do and put a great deal of passion into each and every project.

I graduated from the University of Jönköping with a bachelors degree in Computer Engineering, but my carreer started years earlier. The path to front-end development started when I fell in love with the world of Photoshop at the age of fourteen. I picked up the interest to design for the web and a couple of years later I started to develop my first static websites.

My field of work is ever-evolving, I work hard to keep abreast of emerging technologies and I'm always happy to share my knowledge with others. I carry a strong passion for new technologies, code standards, doing things right, design and intuitive user experiences.

When I’m not working on a project or experimenting with "that new thing" I enjoy traveling, watching football and to just be with the people I love.
